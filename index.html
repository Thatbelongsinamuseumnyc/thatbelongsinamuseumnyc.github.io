About

What is That Belongs in A Museum?

Have you ever had an object that is so personal to you, or has such an interesting story behind it that you wished it could be put on distplay for others to enjoy and learn from? Well then That Belongs In a Museum is an event for you. Based on the idea that Museums should catalouge human life through objects: from the grandest artifacts, to the every reminders and talismans of experinces in our lifes, That Belongs in a Museum gives you the opertinity to bring along a cherished object, tell us why its importatint and have it catalouged online for others to see. Join us for one of our events.
